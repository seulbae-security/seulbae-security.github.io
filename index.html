I am looking for creative and ambitious students who are interested in general cybersecurity, cyber-physical systems, robotics, operating systems, hacking, ML/AI. Please feel free to reach out to me via email to pitch and discuss your ideas.

Research Interests

Cyber-physical systems security (drones, self-driving vehicles, robots, IoT); Test-driven security.

I am deeply passionate about investigating and improving the security and robustness of Cyber-Physicsl Systems (CPS), which encompass practical systems such as drones, self-driving vehicles, robotic systems, and the Internet of Things (IoT) devices.
